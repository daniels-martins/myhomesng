How can I register as a Landlord? +

Go to www.myhomesng.com or download the MyHomesNG app from iOS or Google Play Store.

  • Click "Log in" on myhomes homepage
  • Click "Sign up" on the Log in Page
  • Click "Continue"
  • Fill in your details and Click "Sign up" to complete your registration.
What are the KYC requirements? +

There are two levels of KYC for Agents:

Tier 1: Email & phone verification

Tier 2: BVN verification

Tier 2: National Identification Number and Next of Kin

Tier 2: Indemnity and Proof of Address

Steps to register as a Customer +

Go to www.myhomesng.com or download the MyHomesNG app from iOS or Google Play Store.

  • Click "Sign Up"
  • Select "Customer"
  • Click "Continue," fill in your details, and sign up.
What are the KYC requirements? +

There are four levels of KYC for customers:

Tier 1: Email & phone verification

Tier 2: BVN verification

Tier 3: National Identification Number Verification and Next of Kin

Tier 4: Indemnity and Proof of Address

What are Estates and Communities? +

Estates and Communities is a managed residential or commercial environment/community consisting of Facility Managers and Residents under shared infrastructures.

What is an Estate ID? +

A unique code which identifies specific estates and communities. This is required for Resident sign-up.
